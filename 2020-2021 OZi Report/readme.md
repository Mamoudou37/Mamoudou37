The report draws from data across various regions and agencies to address three main themes:

## ğŸ—ºï¸ Key Findings

### Service Distribution
- **Highest Client Volume**: Trillium Health Partners (435,818 clients)
- **Highest Visit Volume**: LHIN Home Care (4,415,564 visits)
- **Top Francophone Agency**: Centre Francophone (28,410 francophone clients and visits)

### Geographic Insights
- Major concentration of FLHS in the Greater Toronto Area
- Significant presence in Scarborough, Mississauga, and surrounding regions
- Need for expansion in underserved rural areas

### Capacity Analysis
- Majority of staff at Beginner level (648), indicating training opportunities
- Strong core of Advanced-level staff (301) providing leadership
- 91 staff with unknown proficiency requiring assessment
