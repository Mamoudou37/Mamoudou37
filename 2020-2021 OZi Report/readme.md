# 2020-2021 OZi Report
---
##  Overview
The report focuses on the capacity, accessibility, and use of French Language Health Services (FLHS) in Ontario, Canada. It is intended for Local Health Integration Networks (LHINs) and provides data-driven insights into agencies' internal capacities for French language support, as well as patient and client metrics related to francophone health services.
The report draws from data across various regions and agencies, including proficiency levels in French among staff, client volumes, visits, and geographic mapping of FLHS. Key themes include:

Assessing internal agency capabilities for delivering services in French.
Tracking total and francophone-specific clients and visits.
Identifying gaps and opportunities for improving accessibility for French-speaking populations.

Data visualizations in the report include bar charts, summaries, and maps to highlight regional differences and overall trends.
